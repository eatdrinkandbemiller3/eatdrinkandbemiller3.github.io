Top Restaurants in Cheyenne

There are many restaurants in Cheyenne including chain restaurants such as Chipotle, Applebee’s, and Panera. Here are the local ones I consider the best of Cheyenne:

Anong’s Thai Cuisine
Located in the industrial sector, this difficult find is worth the pursuit! They serve wonderful, authentic Thai food in an a beautifully decorated restaurant. The Thai motif provides an atmosphere perfect for a date night or a family night out.
Epic Egg!
A unique brunch restaurant, Epic Egg is nestled downtown. They only serve breakfast, brunch, and lunch. The food is unique, healthy, and full of flavor. Not to mention, the coffee is the perfect wake-up!
Guadalajara Mexican Restaurant
Located near the mall, this restaurant is perfect for dates, gatherings, and family nights. Decorated with traditional Mexican decor, the place is lively. Employees are incredibly friendly and helpful. Food arrives quickly and is full of flavor. Be sure to make a reservation; they fill up quickly!
Hathaway's Restaurant in Little America
Inside Little America Resort, Hathaway Restaurant includes menu choices fine dining, comfort food, and classic variations. Price ranges vary from a typical sit-down restaurant to more fine dining choices. This is the perfect restaurant for a gathering, work meal, or date night. Staff is friendly, and food is delicious.
Mongolian Grill
This little hole in the wall is your typical Mongolian grill: choose your bowl size, add your veggies, pasta, meat and sauce, and watch the chef cook your food on a giant wok. There are many options for flavors as you choose your sauces. The chefs are very accommodating of food allergies, and food is cooked to the perfect temperature.
Poor Richard’s
Looks can be deceiving. You'd never know from the outside that this is the finest restaurant in Cheyenne. The beautiful wood decor provides the perfect atmosphere for a special date night or celebration. Food is impeccable, and staff is friendly and accommodating.
Sanford’s Grub & Pub
Sporting the largest menu and most eclectic array of food, Sanford’s serves "down home food with a Cajun attitude." With walls covered in sports memorabilia and collected antique decor, you will enjoy the fun and lively atmosphere. This restaurant is very relaxed making it great for the whole family. Not to mention their birthday cookie skillet is worth the wait!
Wyoming Rib & Chop House
Providing both formal dining and a bar area, this restaurant is perfect for any dining experience. Options include bar food, a simple burger or sandwich, or a full steak dinner. Food is very flavorful, and staff will make you feel special, especially if you’re celebrating.
