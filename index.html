Explore Cheyenne!

Nestled in southeastern Wyoming and only a few hours’ drive from Denver, Cheyenne is a great tourist destination!

Known for Frontier Days, the largest rodeo in the west, Cheyenne is home to great restaurants, museums, parks, and events. There's plenty to see and do!

Check out the activities and restaurants and see what events are coming soon. Explore Cheyenne!

Cheyenne Boots

Across Cheyenne, there are 25 boots painted by the area’s artists. The theme for the boots was "If this boot could talk, what story would it tell us?" The boots were auctioned at a fundraiser for the Cheyenne Depot Museum Endowment Fund to support the museum.

The 25 boots are scattered across Cheyenne, each with their own story to tell. To hear the story of the featured, original 18, follow the free audio tour at 307-316-0067. Most of boots are displayed throughout this website. Explore and find them!
